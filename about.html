🌟 Our Legacy

Founded in the year 2000, Gandhi Matriculation School has grown from humble beginnings into a beacon of education in Eriyur. Despite financial hardships, our founder A. Kannan stood tall—braving every challenge with unwavering dedication. With 33 years of teaching excellence, he shaped generations and was honored with the prestigious Kali Semmal award for his contributions to education.

🪷 Our Pillar

Our beloved principal Loganayagi Ma’am has been the backbone of our school—supporting every step of our journey with strength and grace. Her leadership helped us celebrate our Silver Jubilee last year, marking 25 years of proud service.

🎓 Our Curriculum & Achievements

We offer LEAD and Astragen curriculum, blending academics with vibrant extracurriculars like Karate, Yoga, Silambam, Skating, Archery, Bharatanatyam, Robotics, Vedic Maths, and Chess. Our students shine in sports too—Throwball runners-upKabaddi runners-upstate-ranked Olympiad achievers, standing tall among many schools.
